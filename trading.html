🤖 CalumBot v3 — How It Works
🌅 Mode 1: London Session Breakout
Active: 11:00am–12:30pm Dubai (7:00–8:30 GMT)
1. Identifies the overnight Asian session range (high/low between 1am–5am Dubai)
2. Waits for price to break above the high or below the low
3. Requires 3 consecutive confirming candles (momentum filter)
4. Minimum range of 10 pips — no false breakout on tight ranges
5. Buys breakout up, sells breakout down
6. SL = 50% of Asian range, TP = 2× the SL (1:2 risk/reward)
🗽 Mode 2: New York Session Breakout
Active: 5:00pm–7:00pm Dubai (13:00–15:00 GMT)
Same breakout logic as London. Second opportunity each day when US institutions enter the market.
📊 Mode 3: Mean Reversion
Active: All other hours (except sleep 1am–11am Dubai)
1. RSI falls below 28 (extreme oversold) → Buy
2. RSI rises above 72 (extreme overbought) → Sell
3. ATR filter: skips if the market is trending strongly (avoids fighting momentum)
4. SL = 25 pips, TP = 50 pips (1:2 ratio)
🛡️ Risk Management
Base risk: 0.5% per trade ($5 on $1,000)
Win streak scaling: +0.25% per level → max 1% after 2 wins
Trailing stop: Moves to breakeven at +20 pips, trails in 10-pip steps
Time-based TP: Closes at 50%+ profit if trade open 4+ hours
Daily loss limit: Bot pauses at -1.5% for the day
Spread filter: No trades if spread > 2.5 pips
Max trades: 1-3 per pair per day (scales with performance)
